About Us

1) Go to Sign Up at the top right of the page, and register a new account. You will not be able to save your score or cart without an account.


2) Sign in with your new account, it will take you to a validated login-page where your username will be displayed in the top right.


3) Each user starts with 10,000 points, and each user is able to wager and win points in the games provided (Currently only RPS is implemented).


4) User points acts as a site currency, user will be able to purchase items from the store with this currency.


5) In the store, add items to your cart, and buy them with your points in the "My Cart" tab.


6) Enter Order form information, order history information will be displayed in the "Members" tab. (Not implemented yet)


7) Games History will also be displayed under the "Members" Tab.
